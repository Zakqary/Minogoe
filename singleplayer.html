How to Play

  • You get one random piece at a time — 70% pentomino (5 squares), 20% tetromino (4), 10% tromino (3). Rotate/flip it however you like before placing.
  • Place it anywhere it legally fits on the board.
  • Fully enclose a pocket of 4 or fewer empty squares and it's captured forever — the pieces that walled it in are cleared away, freeing that space back up. Bigger enclosures don't count, so you can't just wall off the whole board at once.
  • Clear every square on the board to finish your run — the timer stops and your time is saved if it's your best.
  • If your current piece has no legal placement anywhere, the run ends early. Fastest completed times appear on the leaderboard.
