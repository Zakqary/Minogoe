How to Play

  • Both players get the same random hand: 7 pentominoes, 2 tetrominoes, 1 tromino. Rotate or flip pieces freely.
  • Surround empty squares with only your color and/or the board edge to score them.
  • Most points when the game ends wins.
  • You can pass on your final turn.
  • Second player gets a 0.5-point handicap.
  • Misclick? Request an undo, your opponent has to accept it.
